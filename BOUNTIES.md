## Bounty Philosophy

Bounties should pay for evidence, not hype.

Good bounty outputs include:

- stronger classical denominators;
- independent reproductions;
- proof-checkable theorem fragments;
- mature DMRG/tensor-network references;
- hardware or real-backend verifier execution;
- real or curated datasets with leakage audits;
- negative results that close a false path.

## Bounty PR Requirements

A bounty PR must include:

- linked bounty issue;
- B-track and task ID;
- reproducible command;
- machine-readable output;
- human-readable report;
- claim boundary;
- audit result or documented reason audit is not applicable.
